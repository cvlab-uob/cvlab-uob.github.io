Ph.D.

Our lab is always open to talented PhD candidates — there is no fixed limit on the number of students we accept each year. Candidates with publications at top-tier venues (e.g., CVPR, ICCV, ECCV, NeurIPS, ICML, ICRA, IROS etc.) are especially encouraged to apply.

We expect candidates to have:

  • Strong mathematical foundation (Linear Algebra, Optimization)
  • Prior research experience in Computer Vision, Machine Learning, or Robotics
  • Proficiency in Python and deep learning frameworks (e.g., PyTorch)

Having a clear scholarship or funding plan is strongly preferred. Below are some funding opportunities for PhD students:
