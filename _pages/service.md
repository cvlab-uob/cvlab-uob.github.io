### Postdoc

There are currently no open postdoc positions in the lab. However, we are very happy to **support strong candidates in applying for external fellowships** together. If you have a compelling research proposal and are interested in working with us, please get in touch.

**Available Fellowship Opportunities:**
- [Marie Skłodowska-Curie Postdoctoral Fellowships](https://marie-sklodowska-curie-actions.ec.europa.eu/actions/postdoctoral-fellowships) — EU-funded, open to researchers worldwide
- [Royal Academy of Engineering Research Fellowships](https://raeng.org.uk/programmes-and-prizes/programmes/uk-grants-and-prizes/support-for-research/research-fellowships) — For outstanding early-career engineering researchers
- [EPSRC Postdoctoral Fellowships](https://www.ukri.org/councils/epsrc/career-and-skills-development/fellowships/) — UK Research & Innovation funding
- [Leverhulme Early Career Fellowships](https://www.leverhulme.ac.uk/early-career-fellowships) — 3-year fellowships for early-career researchers
- [Newton International Fellowships](https://royalsociety.org/grants/newton-international/) — For non-UK early-career researchers (Royal Society)
- [Birmingham Fellowships](https://www.birmingham.ac.uk/staff/excellence/fellows/index.aspx) — University of Birmingham's flagship fellowship scheme
- [Commonwealth Rutherford Fellowships](https://cscuk.fcdo.gov.uk/scholarships/commonwealth-rutherford-fellowships/) — For postdocs from Commonwealth countries
- [JSPS Postdoctoral Fellowships](https://www.jsps.go.jp/english/e-fellow/index.html) — For researchers with connections to Japan

### Ph.D.

Our lab is **always open to talented PhD candidates** — there is no fixed limit on the number of students we accept each year. Candidates with publications at **top-tier venues** (e.g., CVPR, ICCV, ECCV, NeurIPS, ICML) are especially encouraged to apply.

We expect candidates to have:
- Strong mathematical foundation (Linear Algebra, Optimization)
- Prior research experience in Computer Vision, Machine Learning, or Robotics
- Proficiency in Python and deep learning frameworks (e.g., PyTorch)

Having a clear **scholarship or funding plan** is strongly preferred. Below are some funding opportunities for PhD students:

**Scholarships for International Students:**
- [Commonwealth Scholarships](https://cscuk.fcdo.gov.uk/scholarships/) — For students from Commonwealth countries
- [Chevening Scholarships](https://www.chevening.org/) — UK government's global scholarship programme
- [China Scholarship Council (CSC)](https://www.birmingham.ac.uk/international/students/finance/scholarships/china-scholarship-council.aspx) — For Chinese students (UoB-CSC agreement)
- [Marshall Scholarships](https://www.marshallscholarship.org/) — For US citizens
- [Fulbright Scholarships](https://www.fulbright.org.uk/) — For US citizens studying in the UK

**University of Birmingham Scholarships:**
- [School of Computer Science PhD Scholarships](https://www.birmingham.ac.uk/schools/computer-science/phd/scholarships.aspx) — Departmental funding
- [Birmingham Global PhD Scholarships](https://www.birmingham.ac.uk/postgraduate/funding/global-scholarships) — For outstanding international applicants
- [EPSRC Doctoral Training Partnership (DTP)](https://www.birmingham.ac.uk/postgraduate/funding/epsrc-dtp.aspx) — For UK/Irish students

**Other Funding Sources:**
- [Alan Turing Institute Studentships](https://www.turing.ac.uk/work-turing/studentships) — For AI/data science research
- Government-sponsored scholarships from your home country

If you are interested, please email with your CV, transcripts, and a brief research statement.
